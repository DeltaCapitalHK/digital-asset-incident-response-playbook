# Code of Conduct

We expect respectful, professional, and lawful communication across Delta&Capital public repositories.

Do not use issues, discussions, or pull requests to publish:

- harassment or threats
- doxxing or personal information
- private case materials
- seed phrases, private keys, or wallet files
- instructions for theft, evasion, fraud, or unauthorized access
- requests to bypass exchange controls or legal procedures

Repository maintainers may remove content that creates privacy, legal, security, or safety risks.
