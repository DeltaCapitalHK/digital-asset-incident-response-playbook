# Contributing

Delta&Capital may publish selected public resources related to digital asset incident response, evidence preservation, transaction tracing preparation, exchange contact preparation, and risk awareness.

Contributions should be:

- factual and documentation-focused
- respectful and lawful
- free of private client materials
- free of private keys, seed phrases, wallet files, identity documents, and confidential evidence
- clear about uncertainty and confidence levels

## Documentation Corrections

If you notice a typo, broken link, unclear instruction, or outdated public reference, please open an issue using the documentation correction template.

Do not submit private case files through GitHub Issues.

## Case Material Policy

Public issues, discussions, and pull requests are not safe places for personal or confidential case materials. Do not publish:

- identity documents
- wallet access materials
- seed phrases or private keys
- exchange account screenshots with personal information
- legal documents
- police reports
- private chat records
- customer files
- complete transaction packages tied to a real person

## Tone

Use careful evidence-chain language. Distinguish confirmed facts, suspected links, and items requiring platform, issuer, legal, or law-enforcement verification.
