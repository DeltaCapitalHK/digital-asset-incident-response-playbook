# Exchange Contact Preparation

If on-chain analysis suggests that funds may have reached a centralized exchange, prepare structured materials before submitting a support ticket or preservation request.

## Core Materials

1. Victim identity and contact information
2. Victim wallet address
3. First loss transaction hash
4. Complete fund path before exchange entry
5. Suspected exchange deposit address
6. Amount, token, chain, and timestamps
7. Incident narrative
8. Chat records, platform screenshots, and transfer screenshots
9. Police report receipt, if available
10. Requested action

## Suggested Request Language

```text
We request the exchange to preserve relevant account information and review whether the funds associated with the listed transaction hashes have entered an account under your control.

If the funds are still available, we request risk-control preservation subject to your internal policies and applicable legal or law-enforcement procedures.
```

## Important Notice

Do not fabricate law-enforcement documents, impersonate officials, or submit contradictory fund-flow explanations. If exchange attribution is not confirmed, describe it as a suspected exchange deposit rather than a confirmed fact.
