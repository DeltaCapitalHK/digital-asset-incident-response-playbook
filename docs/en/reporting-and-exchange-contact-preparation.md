# Reporting and Exchange Contact Preparation

Before contacting an exchange, legal advisor, or law enforcement agency, organize the facts into a clear evidence package.

## Before Filing a Report

Prepare:

- Identity and contact information
- Incident timeline
- Loss transaction hashes
- Victim and recipient wallet addresses
- Fake platform URL or app details
- Counterparty contacts and chat records
- Transfer screenshots and block explorer links
- Source-of-funds records
- Total loss summary

## Before Contacting an Exchange

If funds appear to have reached an exchange, prepare:

- Suspected exchange deposit address
- Complete fund path before exchange entry
- Transaction hashes for each hop
- Explanation linking the funds to the victim wallet
- Victim identity and contact information
- Police report receipt, if available
- Requested action: information preservation, account review, law-enforcement coordination, or risk-control preservation

## Suggested Structure

```text
Title: Incident statement regarding digital asset scam/theft on [date]

1. Victim information
2. Incident summary
3. Lost assets and transaction hashes
4. On-chain fund flow
5. Evidence preserved
6. Actions already taken
7. Assistance requested
```
